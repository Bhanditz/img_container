What Types of Slot Machines are available?

A Quick Guide to the Slots available

A brief overview of the various slot machines

  • Classic Slots
    The Old School Classic 3 Reel mechanical slot machine with the ever classic "one arm" handle on the side of the machine. Which is where the nickname come from.
  • Video Slots
    More like a video game nowadays, typically videos slots have 5 or more reels, multiple paylines, bonus reels and amazing graphics.
  • Progressive Slots
    Incremental payouts, bet the Maximum coin value to win the Slots Progressive Jackpot often in the Millions.
  • 3D Slots
    3D Slots are more advanced versions of Video slots, with High Definition graphics and a 3 dimensional look and feel.
  • Cascading Reels
    A very popular slot machine game format with each of the reels being filled by symbols falling from above.
  • Fruit Machines
    Similar to Classic Slots with the familar fruit symbols - Cherrys, Lemons and the Bells for the slots jackpot.
