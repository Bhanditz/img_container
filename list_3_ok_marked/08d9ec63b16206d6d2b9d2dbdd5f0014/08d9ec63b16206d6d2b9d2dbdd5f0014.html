Players Double Their Prizes with DOUBLER As Five New Jersey Lottery CASH4LIFE Players Win Third-Tier Prize

TRENTON (Dec. 19, 2017) – While there was no winner of the CASH4LIFE jackpot or second prize from the Monday, Dec, 18 drawing, five lucky ticketholders win the $2,500 third-tier prize by matching four out of five white balls and the Cash Ball drawn. One of those players added the DOUBLER feature, doubling their prize to $5,000. Moreover 25,315 other players won $142,257 in cash prizes ranging from $2 to $500 with 3,846 of those tickets doubling their prize with DOUBLER. The winning numbers were: 08, 09, 12, 21, and 45. The Cash Ball was: 01.
