It is with great sadness the Lottery League shares the news of losing once of its own with the passing of Derek Poindexter. A member of the band Serlin Spur from the 2016 Season, a friend to all he knew, an incredibly talented musician, and an individual creative force who approached every aspect of his vast involvement within our music community with a sense of passion and dedication that's paralleled by very few others. We couldn't be more broke up about this terribly sad news.

Our deepest condolences to his friends, bandmates, colleagues, family, loved ones, and all those reeling with this loss. Derek was loved far and wide, and he will never be forgotten by all those who were fortunate enough to know him.

Rest In Peace.

Lottery League
· 1 December 2016 ·

It is with heavy heart the Lottery League shares in announcing the passing of one of our own, Alex MacDonald, a member of the 2016 Lottery League season and one of the four members drafted into the band which became known as Solid Circle.

Often over the near decade now of the Cleveland Lottery League we are reminded of just how fun and important our musical art project is and can be, and more importantly, how it has become so much bigger than any individual participant or fa...n, that it is an experience of a lifetime to be shared within an every growing and welcome community of musicians and open minded artists.

When Alex first became sick during our season there was significant concern about whether or not he could continue and perform at The BIG SHOW MMXVI. We couldn't have been more happy for him and his band when he fought through the pain and sickness and still played. His talent, and his joy in participating, embodies so much of what this fun experiment has become - an opportunity for musicians and artists to grow their network, play a show, and work on a project unlike anything they've ever endeavored upon before - Alex embraced every element of his participation and was inspired by his involvement, and in turn, he inspired all those around him.

Alex may be gone but his memory will live on within all those who knew him, and we in the Lottery League consider ourselves incredibly fortunate to call Alex one of our own. Being a member of the Lottery League for every one of the nearly 400 participants and counting who have taken part in this event, for all those who are still with us and for those lost, being a part of this is a membership that knows no end. Rest in peace, Alex MacDonald, you are and will be forever loved.

- The Council Of Chiefs
