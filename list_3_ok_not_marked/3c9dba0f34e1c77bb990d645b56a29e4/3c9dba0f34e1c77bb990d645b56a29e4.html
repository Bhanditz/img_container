Philanthropy

Delta Epsilon has always taken pride in participating in various philanthropic events. Whether with the Makenna Foundation, Adopt-A-School, or supporting fellow Greeks’ organizations, there is a want to give back to the community. We are proud to announce our Spring accomplishments in Philanthropy.

This school year will bring about our 3rd Miss UK event, where we crown both Miss UK and Miss Lexington.  The winners are automatically selected to the Miss Kentucky Pageant in June.  We expect an even bigger turnout and more sponsors than last year.  We also host many car washes at the Shelter while also organizing smaller philanthropy events to help out in our community.
