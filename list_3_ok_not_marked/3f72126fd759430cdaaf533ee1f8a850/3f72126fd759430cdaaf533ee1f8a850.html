Types of Slots – Online & Machines

There are many different slot types so we’ve compiled a list for you to understand the five distinct types:
Classic Slots – 3-reel slots, classically designed. Cherries & the number 7 are extremely popular here. A straightforward game, one pay line.
Video Slots – the modern version of the classic slot. Two major differences: more reels, 5, 7 sometimes even 9 reels, and multiple pay lines, anything from 10 to 241. Also, loads of cool features such as Wild and Scatter Symbols, lucrative bonus rounds and heaps of Free Spins.
Progressive Slots – the machines that attract the most attention in casinos. A certain portion of every bet goes toward an accumulating jackpot. In order to win it you will need to hit a specific pay line or combination of symbols. Until someone gets lucky, the jackpot will continue to grow.
3D Slots – the new generation of video slots. Amazing graphics and animation are being used to give depth and fluid movement to the game.
No download – These days you can play slots without having to download complicated software. Just login to our site and play from your computer, tablet or mobile device when you get the urge to strike it big!
