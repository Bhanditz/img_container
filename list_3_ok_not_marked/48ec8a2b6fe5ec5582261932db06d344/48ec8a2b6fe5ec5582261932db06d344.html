The most popular request we get here at Penny Slot Machines is for more slots that you can find in the land-based casinos. Especially the Las Vegas slot machines.

Of course, as you might expect, many of the most popular games are not yet available online. So, casino slots like Sex and The City and The Great Race are not yet available online, but they may be coming soon

Other really popular games are restricted to UK players at the moment. These include games like Zeus slots, the fantastic Wizard of Oz slots, the novel and very popular Reel Rich Devil slots and the new series of Star Trek slots games made by WMS.

The UK has a government regulated online gaming industry and does not have the land-based slots casinos like in Las Vegas. So, the British online market is very appealing to the makers of the slots - regulation and lack of competition from land-based gaming

The good news for players in other countries is that more and more are following the British lead and regulating their industry. Even in the USA, there are moves to officially regulate online gaming and allow the Vegas casinos, with all of their amazing games to be offered to player in the US that would like to play from home

Right now, industry experts are hoping for the regulations to come through over the next couple of years, with the casinos starting to appear soon after, so there is a lot of reason for optimism.
