Game #1305

Merry Millions

Scratch for Fun

Use your mouse to Scratch For Fun and see an example of how this game is played. Scratch for Fun examples do not award prizes.

Covered View Uncovered View

Game Explanation

TOP PRIZES OF $1,000,000

Merry Millions is a $20 game that offers 5 top prizes of $1,000,000. When any of YOUR NUMBERS match any WINNING NUMBER, win prize shown under the matching number. Reveal a "$" (DLRSN) symbol, win prize shown under that symbol automatically. Reveal a "10X" (10TIMES) symbol, win 10 TIMES the prize shown under that symbol. Reveal a "WREATH" (WINALL) symbol, win all 20 prizes shown! BONUS areas are played separately.

LIMITED TIME! VIP Players Club members can submit qualifying tickets into the Very Merry Second-Chance Drawing for a chance to win up to $100,000! Enter by 11:59:59 p.m. (ET) on 01/04/2018. Find prize details, rules, and enter at palottery.com or via official mobile app. Games may remain for sale after second-chance drawing ends.
