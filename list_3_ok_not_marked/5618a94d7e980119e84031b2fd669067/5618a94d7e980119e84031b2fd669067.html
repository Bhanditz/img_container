MARYSVILLE MAN WINS $75,000 PLAYING HIT 5, EXCITED TO CHECK EVERYTHING OFF FAMILY’S CHRISTMAS WISH LIST

Robert Borleck, a long-time Marysville resident, is $75,000 richer after winning a Hit 5 Cashpot on Monday, Dec. 4. Borleck purchased the ticket at his “lucky store,” the Grove Street Market in Marysville (5931 Grove St Marysville, WA 98270).

The winner told Washington’s Lottery officials that he shared the good news with his girlfriend first, and in her excitement, she dropped her phone and it shattered. He’s already used some of his winnings to replace her phone as an early Christmas present. “Other than paying some bills and putting lots of it in the bank, I plan on using my prize to make sure everyone gets what they’re asking for this Christmas.”

DES MOINES MAN HAS EVEN MORE TO BE GRATEFUL FOR THIS THANKSGIVING DUE TO $180,000 HIT 5 WIN

Thomas Escajeda had big news to share at Thanksgiving dinner this year. Earlier that day, the Des Moines resident discovered he’d won $180,000 playing Hit 5 (half of the $360,000 Cashpot due to two winning tickets). He purchased his winning ticket at Safeway (21401 Pacific Hwy S, Des Moines, WA 98198) for the drawing on Wednesday, Nov. 22.

The winner told Washington’s Lottery officials that his kids have already given him their Christmas wish list and, after some bills are paid off, he’s excited to be able to put those things under the tree for them

PASCO BUSINESS OWNER RELIEVED TO FIND HIS WINNING TICKET FOR $180,000 HIT 5 CASHPOT

A man from Pasco is thrilled to take home $180,000 (half of the $360,000 Cashpot due to two winning tickets) playing Hit 5. He purchased his winning ticket at a local Tesoro (1212 N 4th Ave, Pasco, WA 99301) for the drawing on Nov. 22.

He returned to the gas station a few days later to check to see if he was a winner. The clerk shared the good news and the man hurried to tell his wife. It was then that he realized he had left the winning ticket at the gas station where he’d checked his numbers.
