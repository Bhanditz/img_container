Poker used to be a game for the male generation only. Often found in poker clubs, these gentlemen gather once a week to play poker. Nowadays, aside from being played by literally, anybody, poker at best, offers the convenience of playing it online, thanks to evolution and innovation intervention. What used to be a game for specific adults had become a game that can be played by the young generation and the female generation as well. Moreover, one gets to play with online players from all over the world. Poker has changed a lot since!

 

The advent of technology allowed poker and other casino games to be played within the comforts of your home. This means that you don’t have to dress up to go to a casino or poker club, pay for a cab to get there, buy some drinks and snacks while playing and take a cab back home after the game. Not only you save a lot on these expenses, you also don’t stress yourself too much worrying about the other players or the casino crowd, etc. You can play poker at a convenient time when you feel you need a diversion from house chores if you are a stay-home mom or dad, or just about any time you feel like playing the game. And because it’s online, it’s practically any time of the day or night. If your desktop is in your bedroom, you can play while resting on the bed. The convenience is just too hard to ignore.

 

While some play poker as a gambling form, many who choose to play online poker do so because they want to have simple fun. And the best part is that you can play free poker online – no cash involved; just the game and having fun. But playing cashless poker online also serves another purpose, and that is to provide the player ample playing experience before playing poker for cash. Because playing is allowed at no cost, the player can play as many times as he wants using play money instead of real cash. This gives the newbie players to practice more and enhance their skills at poker without spending any money. Playing poker with play money is not available in offline casino or poker clubs. Beginners will obviously start with small bets as they try and adjust to the feel of the real game. At this point, their confidence is still a bit shaky so they really don’t have to rush into betting big time. As they build their confidence and adjust to the pacing of the game, they will be able to progress into games that will open up more opportunities to win bigger cash prizes.

 

Most casino or poker sites look out for beginner players who log in to their site to play for real money for the first time. What they practice, which most casino sites do, is to offer some sort of a bonus when the player makes their initial deposit to be able to play poker. All poker rooms offer this – a standard deposit bonus, which can be used to build the player’s bankroll. It can also be in the form of a free poker money bonus – an amount automatically added to the player’s account after making the initial deposit.

 

Standard deposit bonus is given to the player after making the initial deposit, or more correctly after satisfying site’s conditions. The bonus amount will depend on the deposit amount. Different poker rooms or casino sites have different regulations and policies on standard deposit bonus. But to illustrate, if a player makes an initial deposit of $100 and the poker room offers a standard deposit bonus of 100%, an extra $100 will be added to the player’s account. However, each site has a maximum limit to the bonus amount. Some sites require the player to play a game before the deposit bonus is released to the player’s account, or other sites have a requirement on the number of points to accumulate to get the bonus money. For this reason, it is important that the terms and conditions that go with standard deposit bonus offered by the site are clearly understood.

 

On the other hand, the free poker money bonus is automatically added to the player’s account without the need to earn or accumulate points or play an initial game. The bonus amount will also depend on the deposit amount, or it can be a fixed amount. This bonus money, unlike the standard deposit money is guaranteed to be in the player’s account right after a deposit is made. But like the standard deposit bonus, the player should make sure that all terms that go with the bonus are clearly understood.
