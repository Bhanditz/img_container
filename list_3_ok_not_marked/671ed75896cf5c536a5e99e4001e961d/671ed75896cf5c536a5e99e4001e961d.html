Also, unlike all the other states offering Mega Millions, California awards all Mega Millions prizes on a pari-mutuel basis, meaning that the prize amounts will vary by how many people won and how many tickets were purchased within the state of California in each drawing. Mega Millions was originally called The Big Game from the game's inception on Sept. 6, 1996 through May 14, 2002.
