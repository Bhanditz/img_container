Poker Variants and Tournaments

We understand that Indian Poker players enjoy variety and that’s exactly what we give them. At Khelplay, you can experiment with the popular Poker games and a host of tournaments.

There are several variations of Poker that are played all over the world. On our website you can try your hands at the 6 popular kinds as mentioned below:

Texas Hold’em
A famous variant in online Poker in which 2 cards are distributed to each player and 5 cards are laid down in a flop, turn and river respectively.

Omaha Pot Limit
Similar to Texas Hold’em, in this variant each player is dealt 4 cards out of which they have to make the best hand using two of them and three from the five community cards.

Omaha Hi-Lo
Also known as Omaha 8-or-better, this type of Poker is becoming increasingly popular as it offers bigger wins as compared to Hold’em.

Blaze Poker
One of the most challenging Poker games online, this superfast variant is the true test of your Poker skills. The most interesting aspect here is that for every new game you will be dealing with new set of opponents.

Razz
This is a type of the 7-card-stud Poker where the lowest hand wins the pot. Each player is dealt 7 cards and the winning hand is the best possible 5-card hand.

5 card Draw
This is one of the easiest Poker game to play online. It is played with blinds or ante as per the Poker rules. The players are dealt 5 cards each, all face down.

5 card Stud
This variant is known as an open card game as a few of the pocket cards of the players are dealt face up. Players here can guess the kind of hand his opponent may have.

7 card Stud
A total of eight players can play this game. Every player here is dealt seven cards each and the hand that hits the pot is the highest 5 card Poker hand.
