Powerball

The next big win could be yours!

Start matching numbers and winning cash with Powerball! This multistate game has an incredible jackpot that starts at $40 million and keeps growing until it’s won.

Want to win even more? Multiply your non-jackpot winnings with Power Play. For an extra $1 per game, your prizes could be multiplied by two, three, fours, five or 10* times! Just ask for Powerball with Power Play.

How to play Powerball

EACH PLAY COSTS JUST $2

There are three ways to choose your lucky numbers:

1. Choose Quick Pick and let the computer select your numbers for you.

2. Choose your own numbers and mark them on the selection slip. Pick five white numbers (1 – 69), plus one red Powerball number (1 – 26).

3. Choose just the white numbers or just the red Powerball number and Quick Pick the other number(s).

You can play your numbers for up to 10 consecutive drawing dates on the same ticket. Just tell the retailer at the time of purchase or mark the Multi Draw box on your selection slip.

Drawings are held Wednesday and Saturday evenings1.

HOW TO WIN

Match the numbers on your ticket to the winning numbers2 selected in the drawing. Match all six numbers to win the big jackpot! See all “NINE WAYS TO WIN” in the chart below.

Check your numbers online, at a retailer or by calling the Winning Numbers Hotline: PHOENIX: 480.829.7425 | TUCSON: 520.325.9141 | ALL OTHER AREAS: 800.499.3798

MULTIPLY YOUR WINNINGS WITH POWER PLAY3

For an extra $1 per game, your non-jackpot prizes could be multiplied by 2, 3, 4, 5 or 10 times4. Mark the Power Play box or ask the cashier for the Power Play option when you buy your Powerball ticket.

CLAIMING YOUR WINNINGS

All Arizona Lottery retailers will redeem prizes up to $100 and may redeem prizes up to $599. Prizes of $600 or more can be claimed at either Arizona Lottery office. Learn more here. Any prize can be claimed in person at the Phoenix or Tucson Office or by mail. Mailing Address: Arizona Lottery P.O. Box 2913, Phoenix, AZ 85062

PAYMENT OPTIONS

If you win the jackpot, you have 60 days to choose either a 30-year annuity or a lump-sum cash payment of approximately half the amount of the announced jackpot. If you do not select cash or annuity within the 60 days, your prize will be paid as an annuity.
