Slot Floor Updates

The slot floor at ARIA is an ever-changing sea of engaging, high-payout machines. Keep up to date on new machines so you can be the first to hit it big.

Anchorman

The Legend of Ron Burgundy™ is now a video slot game at ARIA! This game is jam-packed with entertaining bonus games featuring Ron Burgundy and his comical Channel 4 News team. Anchorman will have you laughing in your seats and spinning for the next breaking story. The central game feature of Anchorman™ is the engaging Big Bonus Ribbon Stop featuring the characters and the Really Good Jackpot. The action, like the news, never stops in this highly entertaining game.

Wheel of Fortune 3D

The most celebrated brand in slots is now the most anticipated new member of the TRUE 3D® Reels family of games. Classic reel themes and the iconic Wheel combine with the multi-dimensional play sensation of TRUE 3D® for an unforgettable gaming experience. 

The Simpsons

Travel to Springfield via Las Vegas with the world premiere of ARIA’s brand new The Simpsons slot machines. They are just as fun as the television show, plus you will have the chance to win enough to treat Lenny, Carl and everyone else at the power plant to donuts.

Walking Dead

The Walking Dead® II Slot Game is a fully engrossing player experience. This all-new game includes the features Michonne Attack, Reel Growth, and Wild Horde. Exciting bonus features include Governor Jackpot Bonus and Governor Free Games. Adding to the post-apocalyptic and gory fun, the game has jackpots startups of $2,500 (Grand), $250 (Major), $30 (Minor) and $10 (Mini). 

SPIN High Limit Room

Designed by Peter Marino, SPIN features unique high-stakes gaming areas that offer a separate casino cage, individual restrooms, comfortable dining area with lounge seating, and a dedicated high limit slot team to elevate your experience every visit.

QUICKPAY is a new technology only available at ARIA that has revolutionized jackpot payout procedures and significantly enhances the guest experience. The technology enables an automated W2G accrual log, which allows customers to service their own jackpots without employee interruption or delay due to paperwork. Not only does this technology eliminate the need for manual paperwork, but it virtually eliminates errors made when manually completing W2Gs, as jackpot events and customer information are automatically stored and recorded electronically.
