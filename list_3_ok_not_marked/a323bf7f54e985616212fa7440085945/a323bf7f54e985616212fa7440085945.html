As announced throughout the month of February the Michigan Lottery Players Club program is only available for Michigan residents effective February 27, 2012. Federal guidelines that regulate gaming activity over the internet are the basis for this important policy.

It is important to note that these federal guidelines only impact internet activities and do not impact the purchase and redemption of tickets at Michigan Lottery retailers. When in Michigan, we hope you continue to visit Michigan Lottery retailers to purchase a ticket for a chance to win cash prizes!

We invite you to stay connected with the Michigan Lottery by visiting your favorite Michigan Lottery retailer, MichiganLottery.com, Facebook and Twitter where you can get the latest winning numbers and more. Thank you for your support of the Michigan Lottery and we wish you good fortune in the future!
