      1. Re: I want to play poker for money.
      Jun 06, 2005, 12:09 PM

      A FEW college students are winning big, a lot are also losing their tuition, etc. For every winner, there is a loser. The money has to come from somewhere!

      1. Hold 'Em for Advanced players

      2. Next step up is 2-4, which is spreada lot of places. Aladdin, Excalibur, Monte Carlo, among other have 2-4

      3. The 'best' room is Bellagio, but the cheapest game is 4-8. My favorite room is Aladdin, because of their high hand mini jackpots and quick drink service. Everyone has their preference though.

      4. I won $2000 in a Binions' daily tournament last year, which was a lot of fun.

      5. All casinos have reasonable buy-ins for ring games, for tournaments try Binions, Sahara and Luxor.

      Good luck............
