Player Rewards Club™ is the official rewards program of both Casino Arizona™ and Talking Stick Resort™. As a member, you can earn and redeem points at both locations, plus you'll receive great rewards like discounts on dining, bonus entries for gaming promotions and more.


How It Works

Earning Points
Simply use your Player Rewards Club Card every time you play table games, keno or slots to start earning points. (Excludes craps and roulette slots.) 

Earn points by playing:

  • Table games - average bet times length of play
  • Poker - earn 500 points per 1 hour of play
  • Keno, Slots - earn 1 point per $1 of play

Redeeming Points
There are many exciting options to choose from for redeeming your points. You can mix and match options any way you choose. Just like when earning points, you can redeem points at both Casino Arizona and Talking Stick Resort.

Ways to redeem points:

  • Cash
  • Free play
  • Gift cards
  • Box Office tickets
  • Gift Shop purchases
  • Meals at our restaurants

Minimum cash out is $5. Table Games points can only be redeemed for gift cards, Box Office tickets, gift shop purchases or meals at our restaurants. Visit Guest Services for complete details on earning and redemption.

Checking Points
Check your reward points balance by logging in on selected Power Perc$TM activated slot machines or by visiting a promo kiosk on the gaming floor inside of Casino Arizona or Talking Stick Resort.

Kiosks allow you to:

  • Check your points
  • Update personal information
  • See offers tailored to you
  • Check in for drawings
  • Enjoy interactive kiosk games
  • Participate in Swipe and Win promotions
