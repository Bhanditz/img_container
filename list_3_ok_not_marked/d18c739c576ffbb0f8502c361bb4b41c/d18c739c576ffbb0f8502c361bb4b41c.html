Best poker sites to start playing online poker

Online gaming has dominated our fast paced lifestyle, allowing people all around the world to interact and enjoy gameplay anytime, anywhere. With millions of software companies continuously bringing applications to the online community, catering to the high stakes gambling sector is top priority.

Poker, the most celebrated card game to date, brings players from all walks of life to take risks and gamble their greens on a table of reading people, strategy, and at the very least, luck. With that said, online poker opened an avenue for amateurs and professionals alike, to enjoy this addictive game without the need to go to the nearest poker room.

Without further ado here are short reviews and need to know facts about what Pokerlistings thinks are the best online poker sites available now:

1. 888 Poker

888 Poker is considered the world’s second largest online poker site. From its 1997 launch, it has grown to become one of the oldest and trusted poker brands in the industry. There are currently around 25 million players worldwide, which was made possible by their software available for download in both iOS and Android devices. However, for one to play on the actual site, the player must be settled in New Jersey; and as with every other NJ poker network, the traffic is reported to be relatively low.

For starters, 888 Poker boasts of its $88 free entry level as one signs up. No deposits required. Variations of the game, such as Texas Hold’em, Omaha and 7 Card Stud are available. Although, it should be noted that only a single table is available for gameplay at a time. Other options like tournaments, cash games, and sit and go’s are also accessible to its players. Players can also customize their character icons, but for an even better playing experience, webcams can be integrated in the game.

888 Poker also brags of its SNAP innovation. Basically, this shortens a player’s waiting time once he/she folds, allowing the said player to be re-seated immediately to another table with a new set of opponents, and continue playing with a newly dealt hand. This whole process is not visible to others, until the player’s actual turn. But it all depends on the player, as one can still opt to observe via the fold watch and play option.

SNAP games can be accessed on the right side of the main lobby screen, further having the cash and tournament sub tabs. Thus, both buy-in games and tourneys are made available, respectively. For amateurs, practice play is also a good option. However, real money is where the big guns are. 888 Poker ensures safety and security verified by McAfee, GameCare, and ICRA. Online payment runs 24/7 with a fast and easy quick deposit payment method. Likewise, for cashing out winnings and money transfer, everything is made easy by just clicking on the cashier tab where the withdrawal and money transfer options are found.

The most attractive asset of WHP is its lucrative loyalty scheme generally categorized into The Points, The Club, The Freerolls, The Monday Top Up, and The Shop. Before you sign up to any poker site

Having listed down nine of the top online poker sites, this removes the nitty-gritty process of elimination and survey on which ones to open an account in. But surely, any of these brands listed can surely jumpstart one’s poker gaming career. With skill, strategy, and a bit of luck, it’s never impossible to earn a sit at the World Series of Poker in the near future.

It's safe to say that we went through almost all the poker sites that are currently available, signed up and played for quite a long time before making some recommendations. 

As usual, we build our top list based on specific criteria such as safety, security, deposit options, software quality, bonuses & promotions and last but not least, the fields that you will encounter (the quality of the players).

Choosing between one of the above is going to be a bit difficult at first but we suggest you study our top poker sites table above and decide based on expert reviews of these poker rooms. 

You can choose more than one, sign up, deposit and play some hands just to see which one you enjoy more and than just stick the best poker site in your opinion.

Poker is one of the rare games where you don’t necessarily want to be playing against the best players all the time.

Most people, in fact, would rather sit at a table with marginal players and hopefully rake in some cash.

This is an important factor to consider when picking an online poker site to play.

Not every site has thousands of players online during every hour of the day, but there are some distinct advantages to playing on a smaller poker site.

Some of the sites have notoriously bad players and it’s worth putting up with fewer players to be surrounded mostly by terrible ones.

Smaller poker sites also tend to do more aggressive marketing and will either give you a better bonus or lucrative rewards as you play more hands. It’s worth remembering that certain sites are known for particular games.
