Experience the most advanced technology, featuring everything from video poker to the latest video reel slots. PAC-MAN™ WILD EDITION

The iconic PAC-MAN character is one of the most recognizable figures in arcade game history and was the basis for the most successful arcade game of all time. Now, PAC-MAN will arrive on casino floors when he stars in PAC-MAN™ WILD EDITION on the new A640® Ainsworth cabinet.

POWERBALL™ 3RM

Everyone’s favorite lottery brand is back - this time in the form of the 3-reel slot machine POWERBALL!

It only takes one watermark symbol to trigger the Power Wheel Bonus for a spin of the top wheel. On the first spin, the wheel only has blue credit slices and a Powerball match bonus slice. After each successful spin, a credit amount is awarded and red wedges are accumulated. Continue to spin the wheel until you hit a red wedge and collect credits, multipliers or even trigger the Power Match Bonus.

Manufacturer: WMS
Location: Casino of the Earth
