Colonial70 Course


The Colonial70 Relay is a 70+ mile race that begins in Hanover just outside of Richmond and finishes in historic Jamestown. It runs on the final 12 legs of the Colonial200 course.
 
The course travels through the forests and rolling hills east of Richmond before joining with the Capital Trail to finish at Jamestown Beach Park - across the street from the Jamestown Settlement.
